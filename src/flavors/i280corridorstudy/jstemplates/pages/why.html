Frequently Asked Questions

1. Will there be a public meeting?

2. For how long will this site stay open for comments?

The map will be open for submissions until October 14, 2016.

3. What happens to the data collected?

All the information collected will be used to identify and evaluate a range of possible transportation-related improvement projects and included in a comprehensive strategic plan for the southern segment of the Bay Area’s I-280 Corridor.

4. Do you delete any contributions?

All spots are subject to moderation from time to time to check for inappropriate content (spots may be edited or omitted).

5. How do I add a spot?

To add a spot to the map, follow these steps:

  1. Click the big green Add a Spot button.
  2. Drag the map to position the at your location.
  3. Complete the form that pops up and press submit.

6. Can I view the bike lanes on the map?

Yes, you can toggle the bike lane layer on/off via the layer button in the top left of the map. This layer is from Open Street Map.

7. What if I make a mistake or want to change something?

If you need to change something, email CrowdSpot (info@crowdspot.com.au) and they'll help sort it out.

8. What is the gray shaded area and can I place a spot in this area?

The gray shaded area is outside of the pre-determined study area for the I-280 corridor in Santa Clara County. You can add a spot in the gray shaded area, but it may not be included in the final strategic plan.

9. Am I required to provide my name and email address to complete the survey?

You are not required to provide your name and email address to the complete the survey. However, if you are interested in receiving updates about the Interstate 280 Transportation Survey your name and email address will be used for these purposes.

10. How will my personal information be used?

Your privacy is important. The information being requested is collected by CrowdSpot and will be used and held in accordance with CrowdSpot’s Privacy Policy. The information may be used for the purpose of providing feedback on the results of this project, keeping in touch with you our latest news, upcoming projects and/or events. You may remain anonymous. If you do not provide personal information, we may not be able to provide you with certain information or assistance. If you do not want to receive updates or to access or change your information, please contact us using the details set out in our Privacy Policy.
