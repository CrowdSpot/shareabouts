Welcome to the Interstate 280 Corridor Transportation Survey

The purpose of this interactive survey is to enable you to share your thoughts and ideas on traffic and transportation issues at specific locations along the I-280 corridor in Santa Clara County.

Your input will help identify transportation improvement projects along the corridor that improve commuting and transit connections; enhance safety and security; support economic growth and sustainability, and is sensitive to the environment and community values.

If you would like more information please contact community.outreach@vta.org.
