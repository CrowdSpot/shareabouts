Welcome to the Interstate 680 Corridor Transportation Survey

The purpose of this interactive survey is to enable you to share your thoughts and ideas on traffic and transportation issues at specific locations along the I-680 corridor in Santa Clara County.

Your input will help identify transportation improvement projects along the corridor that improve commuting and transit connections; enhance safety and security; support economic growth and sustainability, and is sensitive to the environment and community values.

THE SURVEY IS NOW CLOSED FOR COMMENTS. You are welcome to review the comments submitted by other members of the public on this page. If you still would like to submit your thoughts and ideas of how make your commute along the I-680 corridor better, you can send your comments to community.outreach@vta.org until October 24, 2015.
