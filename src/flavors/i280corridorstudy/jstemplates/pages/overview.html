How Can We Make I-280 Better?

VTA is conducting a study of the Santa Clara County’s I-280 Corridor to develop a strategic plan. The study boundaries extend approximately 22 miles from the San Mateo/Santa Clara County border to the I-280/I-680 interchange in the City of San Jose. The study is a collaborative effort with local, state and regional stakeholders to identify possible transportation improvement projects along the I-280 corridor that promote mobility and connectivity, enhance safety and security, support economic vitality and sustainability, and is sensitive to the environment and community values.

You are invited to share with us what freeway, local roadway, bike and pedestrian, and transit improvements you would like to see along the I-280 corridor by completing our survey.
