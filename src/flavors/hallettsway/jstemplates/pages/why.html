Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I add a spot?

To add a new Spot simply follow these steps :

  1. Click the big Add a Spot button.
  2. Drag the map to position the at your location.
  3. Complete the form that pops up (if possible please add an image) and press submit.

2. How long is the map open for?

The map will be open for contributions until 27 June, 2016.

3. What happens to the data collected?

The results and data of this project will be used to inform the design and construction process as we improve Halletts Way. Your feedback will identify issues and opportunities that we need to be aware of and ensure that this project meets the needs of the community.

4. Do you delete any contributions?

All spots are subject to moderation from time to time to check for inappropriate content (spots may be edited or omitted).

5. What if I make a mistake or want to change something?

If you need to change something, email CrowdSpot at info@crowdspot.com.au and they'll help sort it out.

7. Why do you need my email address?

We take your email address so we can contact you directly about your issue or idea. VicRoads wants to maintain strong relationships with the community and being able to contact you about your feedback is important to us.
