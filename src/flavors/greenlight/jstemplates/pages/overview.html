Welcome!

To get more people walking, the City of Port Phillip wants to help make it easier to cross at traffic lights, particularly on major roads.

Tell us where you find it hard to cross the street and why.

Your feedback will help us to advocate to VicRoads to make changes to traffic signals.

Give us your feedback by either adding a new crossing location or supporting and commenting on existing spots.

Green spots are crossing locations suggested by you.

Orange spots are existing crossing locations that we want to collect specific feedback on for the purposes of a separate study.

You can also share your spot on social media using #portphillipgreenlight
