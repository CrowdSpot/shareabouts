Welcome!

We’re creating a long term plan for Bendigo’s public spaces – our parks, gardens, sports fields, regional parks, creek corridors, key streets, etc.

To help do that, we need your input. We are interested to know:

  • What spaces and places are in your neighbourhood (and around the municipality) and how you would rate them?
  • Your ideas for improving existing spaces or creating new ones?
  • How safe you feel in the space you use?

Add as many Spots to the Greater Bendigo map as you like. You can also comment on other people’s spots or give the thumbs up by clicking the 'Support' button.
