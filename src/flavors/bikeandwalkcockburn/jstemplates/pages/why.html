Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I add a spot?

To add a new Spot simply follow these steps :

  1. Click the big Add a Spot button.
  2. Drag the map to position the at your location.
  3. Complete the form that pops up (if possible please add an image) and press submit.

2. How long the map open for?

The map will be open for contributions until 22 May, 2016.

3. What happens to the data collected?

The results and data of this project will be used to inform the City of Cockburn's new Draft Bicycle Plan. The strategy will identify opportunities and strategies to facilitate better active transport integration through the city. Your contribution will help ensure that the strategy meets the needs of the local community.

4. Do you delete any contributions?

All spots are subject to moderation from time to time to check for inappropriate content (spots may be edited or omitted).

5. What if I make a mistake or want to change something?

If you need to change something, email CrowdSpot at info@crowdspot.com.au and they'll help sort it out.

6. Do you have any tips on using the map and placing or supporting spots

Yes, if you click on the Layers button in the top left hand side of the map, you will be able to switch between the Streets and Satellite layers plus turn on the Bike Lanes, Bike Parking and Drinking Water layers. You can easily find your location on the map using the different layers and move around using the mouse. Check that you have not repositioned the map after you have placed your spot.

Have a look at all the spots in your area by switching between List All Spots and Show the Map views by clicking on the box in the top right hand side. In the map view you can see all the spots or filter the spots. You can add comments to any of the spots, hover over the support button and click on it to add additional support. Spots can be shared on Facebook or Twitter.
