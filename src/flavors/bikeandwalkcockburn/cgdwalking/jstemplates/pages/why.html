Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long the map open for?

The map will be open for contributions until 22 March, 2015.

2. What happens to the data collected?

The results and data of this project will be used to inform Greater Dandenong’s Walking Strategy. The strategy will identify opportunities and strategies to encourage and facilitate greater participation in walking. Your contribution will help ensure that the strategy meets the needs of the local community.

3. Do you delete any contributions?

All spots are subject to moderation from time to time to check for inappropriate content (spots may be edited or omitted).

4. What if I make a mistake or want to change something?

If you need to change something, email Anthony (anthony@crowdspot.com.au) and he'll help sort it out.

5. How will my personal information be used?

Your privacy is important. The information being requested is collected by CrowdSpot and it will be used and held in accordance with CrowdSpot’s Privacy Policy. It may be used for the purpose of providing feedback results of this project, keeping in touch with you our latest news or upcoming projects and events. You may remain anonymous. If you do not provide information, we may not be able to provide you with certain information or assistance. If you do not want to receive updates, or to access or change your information, please contact us using the details set out in our Privacy Policy.
