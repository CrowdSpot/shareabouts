Where do you want more bike parking?

Please share your thoughts on where we need more bike parking by either 'adding a spot' on the map or by clicking the 'support' button within existing spots that you agree with.

The results will help inform the university where to plan for future bike parking.

This project also forms part of a students Masters of Urban Planning thesis research. For the purpose of this thesis research please contribute to the map and do not share this email with anyone before the 30th of August.
