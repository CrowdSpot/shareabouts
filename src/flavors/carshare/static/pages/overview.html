About

Hello! You're looking at a demo of Car Share HotSpot Finder. CrowdSpot is an easy way to better understand where there is unmet demand for car share 'pods'.

The easiest way to understand how the map works it to try it out - it only takes a minute. Follow these steps:

1. Add a spot - First choose your location by dragging the map under the marker.

2. Submit your spot - Click the Add a Spot button and fill in the details and press submit. Done!

3. Check out other spots - Click on other spots to add comments, support and share locations over Facebook and Twitter.

CrowdSpot is an map-based tool designed to complement the planning process for public space improvements.

Read more about CrowdSpot at crowdspot.com.au.

Thanks for trying out CrowdSpot!
