BE A TRAILBLAZER!

Discover great adventure in your own backyard.

Explore the Bendigo Creek Trail in Kangaroo Flat. There’s a heap to do and see…take your family, take your friends.

Pin pictures of you, your family and your friends playing, exploring, discovering and having fun on the trail.

It’s fun. It’s healthy. It’s free.

PIN IT TO WIN IT!

It's easy...

1. Find where you are on the map.

2. Click the big 'Add a Spot' button.

3. Fill in the information and/or take a picture of you and your family having fun doing your favourite activity. You can also 'like' existing spots.

4. Click submit and you’re done!

5. *Receive your prize (‘likes’ only are not eligible for prizes)

Be creative!
