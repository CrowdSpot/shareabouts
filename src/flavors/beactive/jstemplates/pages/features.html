Frequently asked questions

1. How long is the map open for?

The map is scheduled to be open until early October 31, 2013.


2. What happens to the information that is collected?

Photos submitted will be used for promotion by the City of Greater Bendigo.

In posting your image and first name, you give the City of Greater Bendigo permission to use these in printed promotional material and social media campaigns.

We need your email address to send you a prize. Your personal details will be kept private and confidential. Please read the privacy statement. Please read our privacy statement.


3. Do you delete any contributions?

The City of Greater Bendigo and CrowdSpot reserve the right to moderate and edit any content on the site. This is usually only the case if inappropriate content is posted.

4. What if I make a mistake or want to change some details?

Email us at info@crowdspot.com.au and we'll help sort it out.

5. How can I stay in touch?

Follow the City of Greater Bendigo on Twitter or ‘like’ us on Facebook or for more information visit: www.bendigo.vic.gov.au/healthytogether
