Frequently asked questions

1. Why is Ararat developing an Active Transport and Healthy Lifestyle Strategy?

Ararat used to be one of Australia's fattest towns but now we're all about becoming one of Australia's fittest towns. The development of this strategy is part of a more holistic approach to improving the health and well-being of our communities. Active transport, whether for transport or recreation, is a simple and fun way to stay fit and healthy.

The ‘Ararat on the Move’ Active Transport & Healthy Lifestyle Strategy is designed to:

  • Generate commitment to increased walkability, cycling and informal recreation and leisure opportunities in Ararat by the implementation of appropriate infrastructure and programs

  • Provide a vision, principles and objectives for walking, cycling and informal recreation and leisure opportunities in Ararat

  • Identify opportunities to provide links between places and spaces in Ararat

  • Identify priorities based on the philosophy of risk management

  • Identify opportunities for destination points

  • Develop management strategies and actions to further improve sustainable transport options for the Ararat community

  • Establish a basis for ongoing cooperation and coordination with and between relevant agencies
