Frequently asked questions

1. How long is the map open for?

The map is expected to be open from 17 February 2014 until 14 March 2014.

2. What happens to the information that is collected?

We hope this map and all the information contributed by the public will create a conversation about the issues and potential opportunities for City Road and help inform future decision making.

3. What if I make a mistake or want to change some details?

If you need to change something, email Anthony (anthony@crowdspot.com.au) and he'll help sort it out.

4. Do you delete any contributions?

All spots are subject to moderation from time to time to check for relevance (spots may be edited or omitted). Your spot will appear immediately so play nice!
