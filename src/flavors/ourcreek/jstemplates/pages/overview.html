  • Environment - Tell us about any spots on the creek with special plants or animals
  • Transport / access – Tell us about where you travel along/access the creek and your ideas for improvements

  • Attractions – Tell us about your favourite places to visit

To add a new Spot simply follow these steps :
  1. Click the big Add a Spot button.
  2. Drag the map to position the at your location.
  3. Complete the form that pops up and press submit.

Add as many Spots to the Our Creek map as you like. You can also comment or vote on other people’s spots by clicking the 'Support' button.
