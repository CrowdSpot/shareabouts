Hello!

Welcome to Cycle Stories 2014: your tales of the best and worst across Melbourne's bicycle network.

Due to the success of the original Cycle Stories project with The Age in 2013 we've decided to keep the project running.

Since only a small fraction of incidents involving people on bikes get reported this map provides a great way for people to share their cycle story.

The results from this project will be used to help create a more bicycle friendly city.

You can still view all the stories and stay up to date with the project by joining our email list or connecting on Facebook and Twitter.

Thanks for sharing your story.
