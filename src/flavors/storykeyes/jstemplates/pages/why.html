Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I add a spot?

To add a spot to the map, follow these steps:

  1. Click the big green Add a Spot button.
  2. Drag the map to position the at your location.
  3. Complete the form that pops up and press submit.

2. Can I view the bike lanes and VTA Bus Route 25 on the map?

Yes, you can toggle the bike lane and VTA Bus Route 25 layers on/off via the layer button in the top left of the map. This layer is from Open Street Map.

3. What if I make a mistake or want to change something?

If you need to change something, email CrowdSpot info@crowdspot.com.au for help.

4. What is the gray shaded area and can I place a spot in this area?

The gray shaded area is outside of the Story-Keyes Corridor Complete Streets Study area. You can add a spot in the gray shaded area, but it may not be addressed in the study. The study will primarily focus on comments made within the first 100 feet.

5. Am I required to provide my name and email address to complete the survey?

You are not required to provide your name or email address to the complete the survey. However, if you are interested in receiving updates about the Story-Keyes Corridor Complete Streets Study your contact information will allow us to keep you informed.
