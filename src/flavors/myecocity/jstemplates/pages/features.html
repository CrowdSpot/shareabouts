Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long is the map open for contribution?

The My EcoCity map will be open from 7 February until 24 Feb 2013.

2. What happens to the information that is collected?

Since you've taken the time to contribute and share we want to keep the the content open as a future reference for all.

3. Do you delete any contributions?

The Sustainable Living Foundation and CrowdSpot reserve the right moderate and edit any content on the site. This is usually only the case if inappropriate content is posted.

4. What if I make a mistake or want to change some details?

Email us at info@crowdspot.com.au and we'll help sort it out.
