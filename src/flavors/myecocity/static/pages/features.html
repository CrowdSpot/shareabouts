Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long is the map open for contribution?

The Bikfest CrowdSpot map will be open from February 15 until March 28.

2. What happens to the information that is collected?

Since you've taken the time to contribute and share we want to keep the the content open as a future reference for all.

3. Do you delete any contributions?

The Bikefest CrowdSpot team reserve the right moderate and edit any content on the site. This is usually only the case if inappropriate content is posted.
