What's your favourite EcoCity Spot in Melbourne?

Melbourne is full of sustainable projects, events, shops and organisations but we've never had a place to capture them until now. The interactive 'My EcoCity Map' was created as a way for the city and its many residents and visitors to share knowledge about all the amazing projects taking place that contribute in reducing our city's footprint.

The map is simple - it only takes a minute to contribute. Follow these steps:

1. Add a spot - Choose your location by dragging the map under the marker.

2. Submit your spot - Click the Add a Spot button and fill in the details and press submit. Done!

3. Check out other spots - Click on other spots to add comments, support and share locations over Facebook and Twitter.

Check out 'The Spots' page above to see the different categories of spots or simply close this window in the top left corner to get started!
