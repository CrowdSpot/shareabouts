What are the Spots?

Project Spot - Project Spots are present for a limited time only. They could be performances, exhibitions or films.

Building Spot - Do you know a building with an excellent green star rating? Pin a Building Spot on in to let everyone know.

Transport Spot - A Transport Spot could be your favourite bike lane or even where you think a great car share spot might be.

Shopping Spot - Who knows where to shop sustainably? Lets make easy for everyone and share the knowledge.

Organisation Spot - There are so many groups and organisations working tirelessly to make life better. Give them some recognition by pinning an Organisation Spot on their location.
