What great places do you want to recognise and promote across Melbourne and Victoria?

Please share your favourites on the ‘Planning Congress CrowdSpot map’. Places could be recently delivered projects, Melbourne ‘institutions’, or a policy, program or initiative that collectively contribute to making cities great. You can contribute to the map by:

  • 'Adding a Spot' on the map with a description, link and image;
  • Commenting and voting on places by clicking the 'Support' button within existing spots already on the map;
  • Parking a question in the bay if you are not from Melbourne and want some local knowledge.

The most ‘supported’ places will be recognised during the 2015 National Planning Congress and act as a great resource for everyone.

Thanks and see you at Congress 2015!
