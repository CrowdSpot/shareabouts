Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long the map open for?

The map will be open for contributions right up until Congress 13-15 May 2015. We will be looking at the map regularly to confirm locations and program details as we lock them in.

2. What happens to the data collected?

The results and data of this project will:

  • Be used by the program committee to make sure we include the best spaces, places, topics and presenters that can in Congress program;
  • Be used by the team planning the social program and so that they know where the best coffee, bars and views are; and where the after parties need to be;
  • Inform site visits, walking tours and other Congress activities
  • Be a real time spatial resource for all delegates to Congress and for members when they visit Melbourne

3. Do you delete any contributions?

All spots are subject to moderation from time to time to check for inappropriate content (spots may be edited or omitted).

4. What if I make a mistake or want to change something?

If you need to change something, email Anthony (anthony@crowdspot.com.au) and he'll help sort it out.

6. Is the map only for place in Melbourne and Victoria?

Yes, at this stage we're trialling this approach and while there are a lot of great places in Australia, we are focussing on spots in Melbourne to help our program planning and capture great ideas about great places.

7. What is my place is in more than one location?

No worry at all. Please just add your place in the most relevant or logical location.
