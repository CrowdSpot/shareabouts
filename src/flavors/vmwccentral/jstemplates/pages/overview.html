What do you think of Victoria's mineral springs?

As part of a new master plan being developed, we are currently seeking you input on what you think about Victoria's mineral springs.

Please share either the spots you like or the spots where you have ideas for improvement. You can contribute to the map by:

  • 'Adding a Spot' on the map with a description, link and image. You can add either 'like' or 'idea' spots;
  • Voting on spots by clicking the 'Support' button within existing spots already on the map;
  • Leaving a comment within existing spots already on the map;
  • Sharing the map with those who you know have a strong connection to V mineral springs;

The most ‘supported’ spots will be recognised during the 2015 National Planning Congress and act as a great resource for everyone.

Thanks and see you at Congress 2015!
