Welcome to Cycle Stories: your tales of the best and worst across Melbourne's bicycle network.

Only a small fraction of incidents involving people on bikes get reported. This is your opportunity to identify the spots where you've had a crash. You can also identify Safe Riding Spots - places you feel particularly safe when riding.

The results from this project will be used to help create a more bicycle friendly city.

You can stay up to date with the project by joining our email list or connecting on Facebook and Twitter.

Thanks for sharing your story.
