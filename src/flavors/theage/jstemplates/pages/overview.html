Welcome to The Age CrowdSpot Project.

Only a small fraction of incidents involving people on bikes get reported. This is your opportunity to identify the spots where you've had a crash.

But rather than just identifying where you've encountered trouble on your bike you can also add Safe Riding Spots.

By sharing your Crash and Safe Spots, you'll help identify the parts of Melbourne with safe and not so safe riding environments.

The results from this project will be used to help create a more bicycle friendly city.

Thanks for telling us your story.
