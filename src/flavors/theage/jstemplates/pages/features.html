Frequently asked questions

1. How long is the map open for?

We expect the project to close on the 5th of June.


2. What happens to the information that is collected?

Since you've taken the time to contribute we want to report back report back to you on any interesting findings that we might uncover. Our team of analysts will examine the data collected, helping to illuminate the views expressed by the community. We will create some data visualisations that identify the areas of Melbourne that provide great and not so great environments for safe riding.

We need to your email to report back to you. We promise not to share your personal details with anyone. Please read our privacy statement.


3. Do you delete any contributions?

The Age and CrowdSpot reserve the right moderate and edit any content on the site. This is usually only the case if inappropriate content is posted.

4. What if I make a mistake or want to change some details?

Email us at info@crowdspot.com.au and we'll help sort it out.

5. How can I stay in touch with the project results?

Sign up to our email list, 'like' us on Facebook or follow us on Twitter.

6. Can CrowdSpot do other on other topics or for areas other than Melbourme?

Absolutely! CrowdSpot maps can be used in any city for an endless list of topics.
