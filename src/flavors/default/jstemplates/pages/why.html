Why CrowdSpot?

Public participation in projects is an increasingly important part of the planning process. Traditional public engagement can be time consuming, costly and only appeal to a narrow demographic of the community.

We provide a practical and intuitive map-based tool designed to complement the planning process for public space improvements. The platform is also great for research and knowledge sharing projects. Some of the platforms key benefits include:

  • Visual and intuitive interface generates greater participation
  • Integrates seamlessly with existing websites
  • Responsive design allows for greater access across all browsers and devices
  • Complements face-to-face community engagement activities
  • Each project results in a collectively generated dataset
  • Visualise results spatially
