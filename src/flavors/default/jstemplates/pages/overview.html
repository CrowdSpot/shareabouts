Welcome to the CrowdSpot demo.

Our maps are an easy way to crowdsource content directly from citizens.

To add a new Spot simply follow these steps :
  1. Click the big Add a Spot button.
  2. Drag the map to position the at your location.
  3. Complete the form that pops up and press submit.

In this demo we've chosen Issue and Idea Spots as examples but the platform can be used to crowdsource information across a range of different subjects.
