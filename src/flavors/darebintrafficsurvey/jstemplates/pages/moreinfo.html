More Information

This June, each household and business in Darebin will recieve a letter notifying them about our Darebin-wide traffic and transport consultation. This survey is a big part of that, and if you would prefer to complete it on paper, please call our Customer Service staff on 8470 8888 and they will mail you one, or you can pick one up from our Customer Service Centres.

You are also invited to attend one of our three public forums will take place in June. These are an opportunity for you to come along and discuss and ask questions about traffic and transport matters that are important to you. The dates and venues are:

  • Wednesday 10 June, 7.00 – 8.30pm - Keon Park Children’s Hub, 1-7 Dole Ave, Reservoir
  • Saturday 20 June 10.00 – 11.30am - Darebin Arts and Entertainment Centre, Cnr Bell St and St Georges Rd, Preston
  • Wednesday 24 June 7.00 – 8.30pm - Northcote Town Hall, 189 High St, Northcote

You can register to attend a forum by emailing civicevents@darebin.vic.gov.au

If you or someone you know would like assistance completing this survey, please contact our Multilingual Telephone Line on 8470 8470.
