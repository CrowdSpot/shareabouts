Welcome!

Thank you for participating in our Darebin Traffic and Transport Survey. We are inviting you to share your thoughts, concerns and ideas about traffic in your street and neighbourhood, and transport across Darebin.

Everyone in Darebin have been invited to have their say and information we collect as part of this survey process will help shape our upcoming Traffic Management Strategy work, and how we advocate to the State and Federal government and authorities for better transport outcomes for Darebin. We also invite you to attend one of our public forums which will be taking place throughout Darebin in June.

To 'Add a Spot' a spot to the map simply:

  1. Click the big red 'Add a Spot' button
  2. Drag the map to position the red cross pin at your location
  3. Complete the form on the right and press submit
You can also comment on other people's spots or add your vote to a spot by clicking the 'Support' button.

To thank you for taking part in our survey, we would like to give you the opportunity to win a $300 Darebin gift pack including passes to our leisure and recreation centres, and movie and shopping vouchers.
