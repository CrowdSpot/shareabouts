Welcome to the Darebin Traffic and Transport Location Plotter

The purpose of this Location Plotter is to enable you to share your thoughts on traffic and transport matters at specific locations.

Your input will help shape the upcoming Darebin Traffic Management Strategy, the works that follow, and how we advocate on priority transport projects. You are also invited to one of our public forums taking place in June around Darebin.

There are 2 different ways that you can contribute to this survey:

1. Add a new Location to the map
This is where you can tell us about particular concerns or ideas you have. Follow these steps:

  1. Click the big red Add a Location button.
  2. Drag the map to position the at your location.
  3. Complete the form that pops up and press submit.

2. 'Comment' on a location on the map
If you click on a location you will see a comment box. Simply type your comment in the box and press the 'submit' button.
