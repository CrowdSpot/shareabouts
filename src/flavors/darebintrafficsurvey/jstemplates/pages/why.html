Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long is the survey open for?

The survey will be open for your contributions until Tuesday 30 June, 2015.

2. What happens to all the information collected?

All the information collected will contribute to the Darebin Traffic Management Strategy and the work that will follow that. We will also be reporting back on these outcomes to the community in August.

3. Why do some roads have green lines on them?

Not all roads within the City of Darebin are owned by Council. For example, the roads where there is a green line are controlled by VicRoads. There are also some private roads, such as those within La Trobe University.

4. What if I make a mistake or want to change something?

If you need to make a change, you can email info@crowdspot.com.au and CrowdSpot will assist you.

5. How will my personal information be used?

Your privacy is important. The information collected will only be used for the purpose of this survey and Council will treat your privacy confidentially. The collection and handling of personal information is in accordance with Council’s Privacy Policy which is displayed on Council’s website and available for inspection at, or from, Council’s Customer Service Centres.

The information being requested is collected by CrowdSpot and it will be used and held in accordance with CrowdSpot’s Privacy Policy. It may be used for the purpose of providing feedback results of this project, keeping in touch with you our latest news or upcoming projects and events. You may remain anonymous. If you do not provide information, we may not be able to provide you with certain information or assistance. If you do not want to receive updates, or to access or change your information, please contact us using the details set out in our Privacy Policy.

6. Do you delete any entries?

All entries are subject to moderation from time to time to scan for inappropriate or offensive content. The result may be that entries are edited or deleted.
