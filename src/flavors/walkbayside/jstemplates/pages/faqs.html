Frequently asked questions

1. Why is Bayside City Council developing a Pedestrian Strategy?

The aim of the Strategy is for Council to better understand the current and likely future needs of the Bayside community in relation to walking and to develop a framework for the effective development management, improvement and promotion of the pedestrian network and environment that will facilitate an increase in the number of people who choose to walk more often as an alternative for short vehicle trips within Bayside.

2. What happens to my feedback?

All of the feedback provided will inform the development of a set of key actions to enable and motivate the community to make more trips in Bayside by walking.

3. How long will the map be open for?

The map will be open for contribution until 18 June 2014.

4. How will my personal information be used?

The personal information requested on this form is being collected by Bayside City Council and will be properly safeguarded and processed in accordance with the requirements of the Information Privacy Act (2000).

5. Do you delete any contributions?

All spots are subject to moderation from time to time to check for inappropriate content that is either inappropriate (spots may be edited or omitted).

6. What if I make a mistake or want to change some details?

If you need to change something, email Anthony (anthony@crowdspot.com.au) and he'll help sort it out.
