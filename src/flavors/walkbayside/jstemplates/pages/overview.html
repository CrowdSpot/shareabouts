Welcome!

Bayside City Council are developing a Pedestrian Strategy to support the community to make more shopping, recreational, education and social trips by walking.

This CrowdSpot website enables the community to spatially map barriers they experience or know of in Bayside.

You can add spots directly onto the map where you face barriers and please also vote to other spots by clicking the 'support this' button.
