About

Hello! You're looking at a demo of the CrowdSpot WiFi public demand map - an intuitive map for you to indicate where you want free WiFi in the city

The easiest way to understand how the map works it to try it out - it only takes a minute. Follow these steps:

1. Add a spot - Choose your location by dragging the map under the marker.

2. Submit your spot - Click the Add a Spot button and fill in the details and press submit. Done!

3. Check out other spots - Click on other spots to add comments, support and share locations over Facebook and Twitter.

Our demo has been setup to allow you to pin spots on locations where you want WiFi Hotspots, just one of hundreds of possible uses.

To find out more visit crowdspot.com.au.

Thanks for trying out CrowdSpot!
