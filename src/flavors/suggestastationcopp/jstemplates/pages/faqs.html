Frequently asked questions

1. What happens to the data that is collected?

The data collected on this map will be analysed, along with other information, to help inform what a priority list of bike share stations may be.

2. What if I make a mistake or want to change some details?

If you need to change something, email Anthony (anthony@crowdspot.com.au) and he'll help sort it out.

3. Do you delete any contributions?

All spots are subject to moderation from time to time to check for relevance (spots may be edited or omitted).Your spot will appear immediately so play nice!

4. How long is the map open for?

At this stage, we expect the map to be open until the end of April

5. Why are the City of Port Phillip doing this community engagement activity?

The City of Phillip has made a decision to be pro-active in involving the community contribute to deciding in the priority bike share stations in the case of a future expansion.

6. Who managed Melbourne's bike share scheme?

VicRoads manage the contract for the Melbourne Bike Share scheme's operations while RACV are the current operator.

6. Will there be a new bike share scheme?

At this stage it is unknown what the future of Melbourne's bike share scheme. The RACV will operate the contract
