Frequently asked questions

1. How long is the map open for contribution?

The EcoCity Food Map will be open from 22 March until 30 April.


2. What happens to the information that is collected?

The data collected will contribute to other data for another project mapping out other sustainable food initiatives in and around Melbourne.


3. Do you delete any contributions?

City of Melbourne and CrowdSpot reserve the right moderate and edit any content on the site. This is usually only the case if inappropriate content is posted.

4. What if I make a mistake or want to change some details?

Email us at info@crowdspot.com.au and we'll help sort it out.
