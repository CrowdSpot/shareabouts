Frequently asked questions

1. How long is the map open for contribution?

The EcoCity Food Map will be open from 22 March until 12 April.


2. What happens to the information that is collected?

Since you've taken the time to contribute and share we want to keep the the content open as a future reference for all. It will stay linked to the CrowdSpot site after contribution finishes.


3. Do you delete any contributions?

The EcoCity Food Map team reserve the right moderate and edit any content on the site.

4. What if I make a mistake or want to change some details?

Email us at info@crowdspot.com.au and we'll help sort it out.
