The Spots

We've got three options you can choose from to categorise your spot. Read the descriptions below to decide which best suits yours.

Community Garden Spots - Do you belong to a community garden? Do you know if there's one in your neighbourhood? If so, mark it on the map and show others where they can get their hands dirty and grow something good.

Local Market Spots - Know where to pick up a good, fresh local produce close to home? Share your favourite local market by placing it on the map.

Farm Spots - Are there any farms nearby? Help support local farmers by showing where to find them on our map.
