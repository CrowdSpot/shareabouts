Welcome to the Interstate 680 Corridor Transportation Survey

The purpose of this interactive survey is to enable you to share your thoughts and ideas on traffic and transportation issues at specific locations along the I-680 corridor in Santa Clara County.

Your input will help identify transportation improvement projects along the corridor that promote mobility and connectivity; enhance safety and security; support economic vitality and sustainability and is sensitive to the environment and community values. You are also invited to our public workshop where you can also give your input, taking place in September.

There are 3 different ways that you can contribute to this survey:

1. Add a new Spot to the map
This is where you can tell us about particular concerns or ideas you have. Follow these steps:

  1. Click the big green Add a Location button.
  2. Drag the map to position the at your location.
  3. Complete the form that pops up and press submit.

2. 'Comment' on a Spot on the map

3. 'Support' a Spot on the map
