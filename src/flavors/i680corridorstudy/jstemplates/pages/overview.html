What improvements are needed along the I-680 Corridor in Santa Clara County?

VTA and Caltrans are preparing the I-680 Corridor Study to develop a strategic plan for I-680 in Santa Clara County. The intent of the study is to identify and address traffic congestion, pedestrian and bicycle access and accommodation, transit connectivity and other transportation-related issues along the corridor.

We want to hear your ideas for:

  • Highway Improvements
  • Bicycle and pedestrian improvements
  • Transit connections
  • Local roadway improvements
  • Noise reduction
