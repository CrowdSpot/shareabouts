We're creating an ‘out of the office’ experience that gives you the opportunity to either present or learn about different projects across Melbourne.

Nominate past or current projects that have assisted the community in experiencing change through engagement practices. This can be a project you've worked on or one you want to hear more about.

There are three ways to nominate a project on the map:

  1. Nominate a new project! Click the Add a Spot button on the map, choose location and complete the survey.
  2. Vote on projects already on the map by clicking the 'support' button.
  3. Comment on projects already on the map.

Get your nominations in before 25 June and block the space out in your calendar so you don’t miss the fun. Check out the FAQs page for more info.
