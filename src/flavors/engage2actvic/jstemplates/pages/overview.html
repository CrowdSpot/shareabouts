Welcome to the Community Engagement and Urban Change: E2A Melbourne bus tour!


Help plan the next Engage2Act event!

Event date: 1pm-5pm, Thursday 30 July, 2015
Event locations: It's up to you!

Rather than choosing a standard event format we want to get out to see how communities are experiencing change and how they are being engaged in the process.

This ‘out of the office’ experience gives you the opportunity to either present or learn about different projects across the city. No PowerPoint, no paper, no office; just a microphone, 15 minutes of fame, bus games and a bus full of networking peers.

You can nominate a project on the map from 3 June – 25 June by:

  • Adding a spot on the map!
  • Voting on the projects you'd like to visit by clicking the 'support' button
  • Commenting on projects

When you have identified your priorities and we have worked out what’s possible within a four hour timeframe, we’ll ask you to book your place on the bus! In the meantime block the space out in your calendar so you don’t miss the fun.
