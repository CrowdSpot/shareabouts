Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long the map open for?

The map will be open for nominations until Sunday 5 July, 2015.

2. When is the event?

The event will take place over 4hrs between 1pm-5pm on Thursday, 30 July 2015.

3. What types of projects can I nominate?

You can nominate a range of different projects. They could be past or current projects, small or large scale projects, complex projects or a project a single overarching challenge. The main requirement is that the project location be inside the non-shaded area and relate to how the community have experienced change through engagement practices.

4. What happens to the data collected?

We're now seeking your feedback to design the event. The crowdsourced information collected will help us plan the four hour bus tour which will visit up to five projects around the city that have been nominated and voted on by you!

5. How will you decide which projects to choose/profile?

Projects chosen for the bus tour will be based on the level of interest in the project (get your 'supports' in) and it's location.

6. What if I make a mistake or want to change something?

If you need to change something, email Anthony (anthony@crowdspot.com.au) and he'll help sort it out.
