Hola! You're looking at Assemble Paper's Back to the Future CrowdSpot map - a celebration of small business heroes who remain quietly triumphant, despite urban gentrification and renewal. Now is the chance for you to share the long-standing institution you love in our city.

Follow these steps to add your spot:

1. Add a spot - Choose your location by dragging the map under the marker.

2. Submit your spot - Click the Add a Spot button and fill in the details and press submit. Done!

3. Check out other spots - Click on other spots to add comments, support and share locations over Facebook and Twitter.
