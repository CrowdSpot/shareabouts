Welcome!

Let us know what’s wrong with your tram, train and bus service. We’re crowdsourcing the problems so that we can get an accurate picture of what needs to be in the plan.

Delays, cancelled services and overcrowding are only getting worse, and Daniel Andrews and the Labor Government have no plan to fix these problems. With your help, we’ll bring your transport issues to their attention.

Use the map to find your spot and tell us your #TransportTroubles.

Add as many Spots to map as you like. You can also comment or support other peoples spots by clicking the 'Support' button. Visit the Transport Troubles website for more information.

To add a new Spot simply follow these steps :
  1. Click the big Add a Spot button.
  2. Drag the map to position the at your location.
  3. Complete the form that pops up and press submit.
