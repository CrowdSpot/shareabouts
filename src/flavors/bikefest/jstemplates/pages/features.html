Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long is the map open for contribution?

The Bikfest Spot Share map will be open from 7 February until 9 March .


2. What happens to the information that is collected?

Since you've taken the time to contribute and share we want to keep the the content open as a future reference for all. It will stay live linked to the Bikefest site, The Squeaky Wheel blog and CrowdSpot site after the festival finishes.


3. Do you delete any contributions?

The Bikefest Spot Share team reserve the right moderate and edit any content on the site. We are framing this as a discussion around the positives of being a bike rider, so negative or inappropriate content will be moderated.

4. What if I make a mistake or want to change some details?

Email us at info@crowdspot.com.au and we'll help sort it out.
