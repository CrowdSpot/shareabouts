About

Hello! Welcome to the 2013 Bikefest CrowdSpot project.

Melbourne has many bike friendly spots but they've never been captured before. Bikefest and the recently launched CrowdSpot have teamed up to try and map our collective knowledge on our cities most loved bicycle spots.

Is there a cafe that you love to roll up to on your pushy or a sweet spot to rest? Add it to the map - it only takes a minute to. Follow these steps:

1. Add a spot - First choose your location by dragging the map under the marker.

2. Submit your spot - Click the Add a Spot button and fill in the details and press submit. Done!

3. Check out other spots - Click on other spots to add comments, support and share locations over Facebook and Twitter.
