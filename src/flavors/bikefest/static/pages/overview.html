About

Hello! Welcome to the Bikefest Spot Share project.

Melbourne is a great place to ride with a multitude of bike friendly spots. We've all got our favourite places, providers and events but there has never been a single map that captured this collective experience. We've teamed up with the recently launched CrowdSpot to map and share the knowledge on our city's most loved bicycle spots.

Make your mark on Melbourne and show your love for riding a bike. We're looking for Sweet Spots, Hot Spots, Beauty Spots and Shop Spots. Go to The Spots page to find their meaning and then add your spot.

Follow these steps:

1. Add a spot - Choose your location by dragging the map under the marker. Zoom in by pressing the plus/minus symbols in the top left.

2. Submit your spot - Click the Add a Spot button and fill in the details and press submit. Done!

3. Check out other spots - Click on other spots to add comments, support and share locations over Facebook and Twitter.

Simply close this window in the top left corner to get started!
