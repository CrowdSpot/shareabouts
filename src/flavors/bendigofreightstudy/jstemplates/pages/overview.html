The City of Greater Bendigo needs to find out how goods are moved around Greater Bendigo.

During a two-phased process, we will gather information from both freight users (Phase 1) and freight providers (Phase 2) to identify practical solutions for decision makers in government and business.

This Phase 1 survey is important because it will help us identify:

  • Current industry characteristics;
  • Freight movement volumes and patterns;
  • Barriers and opportunities associated with enhancing freight efficiency and sustainability;

Phase 2 survey will target freight providers only and will be undertaken upon completion of Phase 1.

You can add two types of spots:

Business Operation Spot: This is a location where you receive or dispatch goods. Please start by first adding this type of spot.

Freight Issue Spot: This is a location where freight movement issues are experienced

To add a Spot simply follow these steps :
  1. Click the big Add a Spot button.
  2. Drag the map to position the at your location.
  3. Complete the form that pops up and press submit.

Estimated time to 'Add a Spot': Approx 5 minutes
