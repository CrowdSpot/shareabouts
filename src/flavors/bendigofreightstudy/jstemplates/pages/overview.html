Welcome!

Thank you for taking part in Phase 1 of the Bendigo Freight Study.

This Phase 1 survey will help us learn about freight moved to and from your current site of operation in Greater Bendigo (i.e. your Business Operation Spot.)

To participate in the survey, simply follow these steps :
  1. Click the big Add a Business Operation Spot button.
  2. Confirm the position of the central marker at your business location.
  3. Complete the form that pops up and press submit.

Estimated time to complete Busines Operation Spot: Approx 5-15 minutes
