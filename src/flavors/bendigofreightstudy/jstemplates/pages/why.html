Frequently Asked Questions

1. How long will this site stay open for input?

The survey will be open for feedback until Monday 14th November 2016

2. What if I make a mistake or want to change something?

If you need to change something, email CrowdSpot (info@crowdspot.com.au) and they'll help sort it out.

3. What happens to the data collected?

The survey data will be analysed and this analysis will be outlined in a Bendigo Freight Study Report. Private information will not be detailed in the Report, which will focus on aggregated survey results and findings.

4. Who will have access to the results of the Study and what will be done with them?

All Study participants, as well as the general public, will be invited to provide feedback on the Draft of the Report before it is finalised and presented to Council. The Final Bendigo Freight Study Report will be a public document available on the City of Greater Bendigo website.

5. When will results of the Study be available?

The final Study results will be available in 2017.

6. Who will the Study benefit?

By seeking to identify practical solutions for decision makers the Study aims to benefit the local businesses and strengthen Greater Bendigo’s economy.

7. How will my personal information be used?

Your privacy is important. The information being requested is collected by CrowdSpot on behalf of the City of Greater Bendigo (CoGB); and will be used and held in accordance with CrowdSpot’s and CoGB’s Privacy Policies. The information will be used for the purpose of Bendigo Freight Study only and may be used to provide you with feedback on the results of this project; keeping in touch with you on the latest Study news and any Study related events etc. You may remain anonymous. If you do not provide personal information, we may not be able to provide you with certain information or assistance. If you do not want to receive updates or to access or change your information, please contact CrowdSpot using the details set out in our Privacy Policy.
