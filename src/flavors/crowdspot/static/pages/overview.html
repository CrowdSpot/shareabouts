About

Hello! You're looking at a demo of CrowdSpot - an intuitive map-based tool designed to complement the planning process for public space improvements. CrowdSpot is also great for research and knowledge sharing projects.

The easiest way to understand how CrowdSpot works it to try it out - it only takes a minute. You can put spots on the map, add comments, vote, discuss other people's suggestions and share locations over Facebook and Twitter.

Our demo has been setup to allow you to pin spots on locations for Bike Share Docks, WiFi Hotspots and Public Toilets - just three of hundreds of possible uses.
