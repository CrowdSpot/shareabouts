About

Hello! You're looking at a demo of CrowdSpot.

CrowdSpot is a simple web app for crowdsourced mapping. You can use it to collect public input on maps - for example, where should a new park be built? Which bus stops need a new shelter? People can put spots on the map, add comments, vote, discuss other people's suggestions and share locations over social networks.

Read more about CrowdSpot at crowdspot.com.au.

Thanks for trying out CrowdSpot!
